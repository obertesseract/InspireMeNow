Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 • Effective Date: November 22, 2025

Ober Tesseract ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the InspireMe Now mobile application. We operate in compliance with the LGPD (Brazil), GDPR (Europe), and other applicable global privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide our services effectively:

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Data: Email address (required for account creation) and Name (optional).
  • Profile Data (Optional): Gender, Location (City/Country).
  • Sensitive Data (Special Category):
    • Religious Beliefs: Used strictly to customize spiritual content.
    • Emotional State: Data regarding your mood (e.g., "anxious," "grateful") and check-in scores.
    • Journal Entries: The personal text you write in your diary.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent, favorites saved, biblical passages read, and feature usage statistics.
  • Technical Data: Device model, operating system version, IP address, unique device identifiers, and crash logs.
  • Advertising Data: If you use the free version, Google AdMob collects data (like Advertising ID) to show relevant ads.

2. How We Use Your Data

Purpose Legal Basis (GDPR/LGPD)
Service Provision (Login, saving progress) Contractual Necessity
Personalization (Selecting Bible verses) Explicit Consent
Customer Support Legitimate Interest
Advertising (Free users only) Consent / Legitimate Interest
Subscription Management Contractual Necessity

3. Sensitive Data Handling

We treat your Religious Beliefs and Emotional Data with extra care. We process this data solely to provide the personalized core functionality of the App (e.g., showing a Bible verse for anxiety). We do not sell this sensitive data to third parties.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted third-party service providers required to operate the App:

Google Firebase For authentication, database storage (Firestore), and notifications.
RevenueCat To validate your subscription receipts with the App Store/Google Play.
Google AdMob To display advertisements (Free plan only).
Notifee To handle local notification scheduling on your device.

5. International Data Transfers

The App is operated from Brazil, but our service providers (Google/Firebase) maintain servers globally, primarily in the United States. By using the App, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to countries with different data protection laws. We ensure these providers adhere to strict security standards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses).

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We and our partners use identifiers (like the Android Advertising ID or iOS IDFA) to collect information.

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential for the app to function.
  • Analytics: Help us understand how you use the app.
  • Advertising: Used to show ads relevant to your interests.

You can reset your advertising ID in your device settings at any time.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data from our active databases within 30 days, unless legal obligations require longer retention.

8. Your Rights (LGPD & GDPR)

Depending on your location (Brazil, EU, or California), you have specific rights:

Access & Portability Request a copy of the data we hold about you in a structured format.
Rectification Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
Deletion (Right to be Forgotten) Request deletion of your account and data directly inside the App settings.
Withdraw Consent Revoke consent for notifications or data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights: Please use the "Delete Account" button in the app settings or contact us at obertesseract@gmail.com.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (such as HTTPS/TLS encryption and secure Firebase authentication) to protect your data. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.

12. Contact Us / Data Protection Officer

If you have questions about your privacy, this policy, or wish to exercise your rights, please contact our team.
