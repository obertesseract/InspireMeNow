Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ober Tesseract ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with the InspireMeNow mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

  • User Input (Emotional State): We collect the emotional state or feeling (e.g., anxiety, fear, loneliness) you select or input to generate personalized content. This data is handled pseudonymously.
  • Generated Content: The reflections and scripture snippets generated by our AI service and displayed in the App are stored in Firebase Firestore to maintain your reading history and preferences across devices.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal data about how you interact with the App (e.g., features accessed, duration of use) for optimization purposes.
  • Authentication Data: We use Firebase Authentication to securely manage user accounts, which may involve unique identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the InspireMeNow App.
  • Generate personalized, multi-lingual emotional and spiritual reflections using integrated AI based on your input.
  • Store your reading history and settings via Firebase Firestore for a seamless multi-platform experience.
  • Improve our services and develop new features based on aggregated, anonymized usage data.

3. Data Storage and Security (Firebase Firestore)

We leverage Google's Firebase Firestore for secure, cloud-based data storage. While the data is stored securely, it is important to understand that:
a) We do not collect or store sensitive personal identifiers such as names or emails for the content generation process.
b) Your emotional inputs are transiently used by the AI model to generate the reflection, which is then stored under your unique user ID.
